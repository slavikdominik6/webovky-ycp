We are open to cooperation on Erasmus+ youth exchanges and training courses with organisations across Europe.

Who we are

Youth Center Prague is a Czech social enterprise and student cooperative based in Prague. We are closely connected to Tiimiakatemia Prague and operate as a learning community of students, mentors, and youth workers who prioritize experiential education.

We are currently operating as a sending organisation, supporting young people from the Czech Republic in participating in international Erasmus+ projects. We are actively building our hosting capacity and working toward establishing long-term partnerships across Europe.

Our team has hands-on experience with Erasmus+ projects as participants, team leaders, and youth mentors — we know what makes a project meaningful.

How we work

Reliable. Direct. Honest.

What you can expect from us as a partner organisation — practical things, no marketing fluff.

01 · Communication

📨

Replies in days, not weeks

We respond to partner emails within 2–4 working days, even during summer. If we can't commit, we say so quickly so you can plan around us.

02 · Participants

🎯

Selected, briefed, prepared

Our participants don't just "sign up" — they go through a short motivation form and a pre-departure briefing. They arrive ready to engage.

03 · Documentation

📋

Mobility Tool · Youthpass · Reports

We handle our side of the paperwork on time. Travel reimbursement, Mobility Tool entries and final reports — all in by the deadline.

04 · Promotion

📣

Real social reach in Czechia

Active Instagram & TikTok focused on Czech youth. Project promotion goes beyond "we shared the link" — we tell the story.
